Package FAQ

Questions about the tiers

Can I upgrade after placing an order?

Yes. If you need a broader scope after receiving your initial report, contact us within 7 days. We will apply your original payment as credit toward the higher tier and issue a differential invoice for the balance.

Which tier is best for probate attorneys?

The Probate & Heirship Investigation Report (Tier 03) is purpose-built for estate proceedings — it focuses on decedent identification, property connection, death indicators, and heir candidate discovery with contactability scoring.

Does the delivery window include weekends?

Delivery windows are calendar days, not business days. Our automated research engine operates continuously. Delays may occur for complex subjects or county portal outages, which are documented in your report.

What if I need coverage in multiple counties?

Most tiers can search across multiple counties. If your case spans many jurisdictions, the Custom Research tier is designed for multi-county and multi-state investigations with a bespoke scope.

Are reports admissible as legal evidence?

TraceWorks reports are investigative research tools, not certified legal documents. They are intended to support legal professionals in identifying leads, sources, and findings for further verification and formal proceedings.

How is the Comprehensive tier different from buying Tiers 1–4?

The Comprehensive tier runs all sources in a unified investigation, applies cross-source discrepancy analysis to resolve conflicts between findings, produces a confidence matrix, and delivers strategic next-step recommendations — none of which appear in individual tier reports.
