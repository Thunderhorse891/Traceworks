          Enterprise Inquiry

          Scope & Clarity

          Common Questions

          Straightforward answers about what the reports include, what they don't, and when manual review is needed.

          What's included in every report?

          Every report includes a Source Trace Panel — a table of every source queried, the exact query used, the timestamp, and the result status (Found, Not Found, Unavailable, or Error). No source is silently omitted. If a source couldn't be reached, it is documented with a direct manual-review link.

          What is not guaranteed?

          These are public-record investigative reports, not title commitments, heirship certifications, or legal opinions. We cannot guarantee completeness of county indexes, availability of any specific source, or that all properties, entities, or heirs associated with a subject will be found. Every limitation is labeled in the report.

          How long does delivery take?

          Most reports complete within 10–45 minutes of confirmed payment. Complex subjects or unavailable county portals may extend this. Comprehensive tier reports may take up to 90 minutes. You will receive an email when the report is ready — no need to wait on the confirmation page.

          What if a source is unavailable?

          Sources that require browser authentication (such as county deed indexes and TX Courts Online) are documented as "Unavailable — Manual Review Required" with a direct link to the source for human follow-up. We never fabricate data when automation fails. You receive an honest accounting of exactly what was accessible.

          What is the refund policy?

          No refunds after research begins — which occurs immediately after payment confirmation. If findings are incomplete due to source unavailability, we run a redo pass on the same scope at no charge. See the full No-Refund Redo Policy for scope definition.

          Who is this service for?

          TraceWorks is for licensed legal professionals, real estate attorneys, probate investors, title researchers, process servers, and asset recovery firms. This service is for lawful investigative and legal professional use only. It is not a consumer reporting service under FCRA and must not be used for credit, employment, or tenant screening decisions.
