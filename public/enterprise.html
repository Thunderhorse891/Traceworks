Enterprise FAQ

Can we integrate TraceWorks into our case management system?

Enterprise clients can discuss API integration options. The current system delivers reports via email; webhook delivery and API endpoints can be built for qualified enterprise accounts.

Do enterprise reports include states outside Texas?

Current coverage is Texas-focused. Multi-state connector builds are available for enterprise clients — coverage scope is agreed upon during onboarding and typically adds 4–8 weeks for connector development.

How does billing work for enterprise plans?

Enterprise clients receive monthly invoices. Payment via ACH or wire transfer. Credit card available for Agency tier. All pricing is per-cycle — unused report volume does not roll over.

Are reports admissible as evidence?

TraceWorks reports are research artifacts, not legal opinions or court filings. They cite publicly available sources with full source attribution. Admissibility is determined by your jurisdiction and legal counsel — we make no representations about evidential use.
